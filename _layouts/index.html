Three Pillars of Scrum

  • Transparency: The emergent process and work must be visible to those performing the work.
  • Inspection: Inspection enables adaptation. Inspection without adaptation is considered pointless.
  • Adaptation: A Scrum Team is expected to adapt the moment it learns anything new through inspection.

Virtuous Scrum: SCRUMtuous

Successful use of Agile and Scrum depends on people becoming more proficient in living five values:

  • Commitment: The act of committing, pledging, or engaging oneself.
  • Focus: A central point, as of attraction, attention, or activity.
  • Openness: The quality or state of being relatively free from obstruction.
  • Respect: Esteem for or a sense of the worth or excellence of a person.
  • Courage: The quality of mind or spirit that enables a person to face difficulty or danger without fear.
